What is the Decentral Card Network?

The Decentral Card Network (DCN) combines two of your most beloved things:
1) having an opinion and...
2) playing card games.

We plan on giving you a way to express both of these by giving the player, that is you, the opportunity to actively create the game by envisioning new cards, judging other people's cards and test them in real games against our AI or human players. Bla bla bla

We greatly appreciate any suggestions, help or feedback in general!
So, feel more than free to join our Telegram group.
